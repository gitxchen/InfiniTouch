- This kernel is only for the LG Nexus 5 (hammerhead)
- Flash TWRP using fastboot first 
- Then use TWRP to flash the kernel. Instructions: https://twrp.me/lg/lgnexus5.html

Capacitive images are written to /proc/capacitive_matrix. You can use libftsp.jar as described in the README.MD to access the capacitive images in your own Android application.
